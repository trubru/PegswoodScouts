About Pegswood Scouts

Pegswood Scouts is a thriving scout group with a Beaver and Cubs sections.

We provide fun and adventure for over 30 boys and girls in Pegsood. We have two sections: Bevers for 6 to 8 year olds and Cubs for 8 to 10½. Opertunities will be available for children to join Scouts from 10½ year old in Morpeth.

We are part of the 4th Morpeth Scout Group; a large and popular Scout group in Morpeth.

Where do we meet?

We meet weekly in school term time at Pegswood Primary School.

  • Beavers meet from 5.30pm until 6.30pm.
  • Cubs meet from 6.45pm until 7.55pm.

Leaders

Christine Crudden is the overall leader for both the Beavers and Cubs sections with Stuart Holmes assuming the role of Assistant Leader for the Cubs.

Adult helpers and Section Assistants

Adult helpers and Section Assistants play a cruicial role in helping with activities and meetings helping ensure that our sessions are fun and safe.

We require at least one helper per session. If you would like to join in, please speak to Christine or Stuart at one of our meetings, or get in touch.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does my child join?

If your child would like to join, please get in touch. We run taster sessions from time-to-time and your child would be more than welcome to join in to see if they like it.

We welcome both boys and girls to our sections - Beavers are for 6-8 year olds and Cubs for 8-10 year olds.

Can I help?

Adult helpers and Section Assistants play a cruicial role in helping with activities and meetings helping ensure that our sessions are fun and safe.

We require at least one helper per session. If you would like to join in, please speak to Christine or Stuart at one of our meetings, or get in touch.

Does my child require a uniform?

All beavers and cubs are required to wear a uniform when they are invested into their Section. The uniform is made up of an offical section jumper and knecker - Beavers wear a light blue jumper and Cubs a green jumper. Kneckers and badges are provided when the child is invested and as they earn them and all badges are sewn onto their jumper.

Jumpers is available to purchase from the Northumberland Scout Shop or you can purchase from John Lewis' Newcastle Store or online. An offical polo shirt is available to wear underneth the jumper if required.

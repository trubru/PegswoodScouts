Volunteer

Our leaders and helpers all volunteer their time to make our sessions fun and safe.

Help children learn valuable skills or support us at camp or events. Volunteering is ideal for developing your own skills and can look great on your CV.

If you would like to volunteer, please talk to one of the leaders at one of our sessions or contact us.

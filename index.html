Volunteer

Our Cub and Beaver sections at Pegswood are growing.

Scouts are run by volunteers. Wether you want to join in the weekly meetings, bake cakes for coffee mornings, or help out at sleepovers and camps, you can help as little or as often as you like.

Why Volunteer

We need adults to support our leaders in delivering a programme that is fun and safe for the children.

“ Working with the Beavers and Cubs is more fun than I expected; the energy from the kids is infectious! Being involved is helping me build my confidence and leadership skills.”

— Stuart, Assistant Cub Leader

We are looking to recruit:

  • Assistant Leaders - uniformed members who are more involved with planning and the running of meetings,
  • Section Assistants - uniformed leaders to help with meetings,
  • Occassional Helpers - adults who help on an occasional basis.
